Your Routine for a Radiant Glow

Simple steps to achieve a luxurious, silky-soft feeling every day.

1. Polish with the Body Scrub

Apply to damp skin and gently massage in circular motions, focusing on rough areas. Rinse with warm water. Use 1-2 times a week for a silky-smooth base.

2. Hydrate with the Body Lotion

Apply a generous amount to clean, dry skin. Gently massage in circular motions until fully absorbed. Use daily for best results.

3. Enhance with the Tanning Oil

Apply generously to clean, dry skin before sun exposure. Massage evenly in circular motions. Reapply regularly, especially after swimming, to maintain your golden glow.
